Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon installation

🛡️

ShopSense AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We only read publicly visible product data from shopping pages you actively visit, and only when you open the ShopSense AI panel.

1. What We Read (Not Collect)

When you open ShopSense AI on a supported product page, the extension reads the following publicly visible information directly from the webpage DOM:

Data FieldPurposeStored?
Product name & URLIdentify the product being analysedLocal cache only, 24hr TTL
PricePrice/value scoringNo
Star rating & review countRatings quality scoringNo
Customer review textReview authenticity & insight generationNo
Product specificationsSpec completeness scoringNo
Seller name, delivery, return policyDelivery & brand scoringNo

This data is sent to our backend API only to generate your analysis and is not logged, retained, or associated with any identity.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information.
  • We do not track your browsing history or which pages you visit.
  • We do not collect purchase history, payment information, or shopping cart contents.
  • We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any cross-site tracking.
  • We do not share any data with advertisers or third-party analytics services.
  • We do not access microphone, camera, clipboard, or any device hardware.

3. Local Cache

Analysis results are stored in your browser's chrome.storage.local for 24 hours so revisiting the same product loads instantly without an additional API call. This data never leaves your device and is automatically purged after 24 hours.

You can clear this cache at any time by clicking the refresh button in the ShopSense AI panel or by removing the extension.

4. Backend API

ShopSense AI uses a proxy backend (hosted on Railway / Vercel) to forward product data to the Groq AI API. This proxy:

  • Receives only the product data listed in Section 1 — no user identifiers are sent.
  • Does not log request bodies to disk.
  • Uses a per-session in-memory cache that is automatically cleared on server restart.
  • Is rate-limited using the anonymous Chrome extension ID (not a personal identifier) to prevent abuse.

ShopSense AI is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
